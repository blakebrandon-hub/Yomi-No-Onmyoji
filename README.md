> *"The Shogunate fights with steel. You fight with the souls of the dead."**

The Yomi-gates have torn open. Twisted, bleeding Torii are spreading across the provinces, vomiting hordes of Oni and corrupted Yokai into an alternate, dark-fantasy Edo Japan. The Shogun’s armies are being slaughtered, their silver-mining towns and bamboo groves reduced to cursed ash. 

You are Morikage, a veteran death-priest of an ancient, forbidden sect of Onmyoji. While the samurai die in vain trying to slay the demonic vanguard, you possess a darker, heretical genius: you do not merely fight the demons. 

You bind them. 

**Yomi no Onmyoji** is a dynamic, AI-driven text RPG where dark fantasy meets Japanese folklore. Step into a gritty, synth-infused interface and command the forces of the underworld to save a realm that fears you.

### 💀 CORE FEATURES:

*   **Command the Fallen:** Resurrect fallen ashigaru, wandering ronin, and slaughtered peasants to build your army. Every corpse you raise is unique, retaining a haunting, fragmented memory of their past life. They will hold the line for you—until their second death.
*   **Enslave the Abyss:** Why destroy an Oni when you can weaponize it? Weaken lesser Yokai and use forbidden blood-sutras to bind them to your will. Bound demons are incredibly powerful, but they remember what they are, and they resent the leash. Push them too hard, and they will break free.
*   **A Living, AI-Driven Chronicle:** Powered by an advanced AI narrator, every action you type dynamically shapes the world. Talk to villagers, negotiate vows (quests), and describe your combat maneuvers in natural language. The game reacts seamlessly to your choices.
*   **Master Forbidden Arts:** Manage your Vitality and Soul Energy to cast devastating spells from your Grimoire. Unleash *Necrotic Bursts*, siphon hellfire from burning demons, or armor yourself in shattered bone. But beware—overusing the dark arts courts physical collapse.
*   **Dynamic World State:** Track your standing across four distinct, corrupted regions—from the haunted cedar forests of *Kuro-mori* to the arrogant, denial-stricken Imperial Capital of *Miyako*. Watch the map update dynamically as the fog of war shifts. 
*   **Dark Edo Atmosphere:** Immerse yourself in a beautifully grim UI featuring real-time generative scene visualization, ambient soundscapes, and text-to-speech narration that brings the horror of the Yomi-gates to life.

The gates are bleeding. The Yokai are starving. To save the living, you must become the master of the dead. 

## Summoning
- **Yomi Call** — Animates a recently slain human or mundane corpse. Requires a body.  
- **Call Shikigami** — Summons a bound paper-spirit for scouting or communion. Cannot fight.  
- **Bind Yurei** — Enslaves a free-roaming ghost to Morikage's will.  
- **Mass Rising** — Raises multiple corpses at once. Catastrophic if rushed.  

## Corruption
- **Soul Drain** — Leaches life-force from a target, restoring Morikage's HP slightly.  
- **Wither** — Decays flesh and rusts armor, weakening a living enemy over time.  
- **Kegare Siphon** — Turns an oni's own hellfire against it. Only works on flame-type demons.  
- **Necrotic Burst** — Explosive release of death-energy and cursed ash. Damages all nearby.  

## Warding
- **Death Ward** — Marks Morikage against one incoming killing blow.  
- **Seal Torii** — Partially closes or destabilizes a Yomi-gate. Temporary without a Shinto ritual.  
- **Corpse Armor** — Wraps Morikage in animated, shattered lamellar and bone. Reduces HP loss.  
- **Yokai Ward** — Creates a salt-and-blood barrier demons cannot willingly cross. Exhausting to maintain.  

## Mastery
- **Soul Echo** — Reads the last memory of a corpse. Useful for intelligence.  
- **Spirit Harvest** — Extracts and stores soul energy from dying creatures using a gourd.  
- **Bind Oni** — The forbidden binding rite. Strips a weakened demon's will via a nailed talisman. Requires the demon be below ~30 HP.  
- **Zan-Kuroda's Bane** — A weapon of last resort, forged in the shadow-order's final days. Morikage cannot cast this lightly.
